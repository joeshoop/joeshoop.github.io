Julep Maven is a monthly box full of high quality nail polishes and other beauty products. Julep was expanding their subscription with new tiers, giving subscribers more options to customize the items they would recive in their upcoming monthly box, and they needed a new app.

I worked with Toy Rockets to design a new app for Julep that included this upgraded flexibility and helped clarify how to take advantage of these new features.
